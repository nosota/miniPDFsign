# Privacy Policy — PDFSign

**Last updated:** [insert date]

Your privacy is important. PDFSign is designed to operate fully offline and to minimize data exposure.

## 1. Data Collection
PDFSign does **not collect, store, or transmit** any personal data.

The application does not collect:
- Personal information (name, email, address, identifiers)
- Documents opened in the app
- File contents or metadata
- Images of signatures or stamps
- Usage data
- Analytics data
- Tracking data

## 2. Local Processing Only
All files, documents, and signature images are processed and stored **locally on your device**.  
No data is uploaded to cloud services or external servers.

## 3. No Tracking
PDFSign does not use:
- Advertising frameworks
- Tracking technologies
- Third-party analytics SDKs

## 4. Third-Party Access
Because PDFSign does not collect user data, no personal data is shared with third parties.

## 5. Children’s Privacy
PDFSign does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.

## 6. Changes to This Policy
If this Privacy Policy is updated, the revised version will be made available within the application or on the official website.
