## Description (English)

Sign and stamp your PDF documents in seconds — completely offline and private.

miniPDFSign is a simple, focused tool for adding visual signatures, stamps, and images to PDF files. No accounts, no cloud, no tracking. Your documents stay on your device.

**KEY FEATURES**

• Add signatures and stamps to any PDF
• Build your personal library of signatures and stamps
• Drag and drop images onto PDF pages
• Resize and rotate with intuitive gestures
• Pinch to zoom, scroll through multi-page documents
• Works with iOS Files app and Share Sheet
• Fully offline — no internet required
• No accounts or sign-up needed
• Supports 66 languages including RTL (Arabic, Hebrew, Persian)

**HOW IT WORKS**

1. Open a PDF from Files, email, or any app
2. Add your signature or stamp images to the library
3. Drag them onto the document
4. Resize and position as needed
5. Save or share the signed PDF

**PRIVACY FIRST**

miniPDFSign processes everything locally on your device. We don't collect any data — no analytics, no tracking, no cloud uploads. Your signatures and documents are yours alone.

**PERFECT FOR**

• Signing contracts, agreements, and forms
• Adding company stamps and logos
• Marking documents as reviewed or approved
• Quick annotations and visual notes
• Anyone who values privacy and simplicity

**IMPORTANT NOTE**

miniPDFSign adds visual elements only. It does not provide cryptographic digital signatures or legally certified e-signatures. The legal effect of visual signatures depends on applicable laws and agreements between parties.
