PDFSign — Visual Signatures & Stamps for PDF

PDFSign is a lightweight tool for adding visual signatures, stamps, and images to your PDF documents.

Features:
•	Store unlimited signature and stamp images
•	Quickly insert signatures into any PDF
•	Drag & drop support
•	Works fully offline
•	No cloud, no accounts, no tracking
•	Your files stay on your device

Important note:

PDFSign adds visual elements only. It does not provide cryptographic or certified electronic signatures. The legal effect of visual signatures depends on applicable law and agreements between the parties.

Perfect for:
•	Signing contracts visually
•	Adding company stamps
•	Annotating documents
•	Filling and marking PDFs

Simple, private, and fully under your control.
